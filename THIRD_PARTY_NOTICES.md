## Bundled diagram fonts

The extension includes the normal 400 weight, Latin and Latin Extended WOFF2
files for Inter and Noto Sans. The files were obtained from Fontsource commit
[`1fdb61d`](https://github.com/fontsource/font-files/commit/1fdb61d90d0e5a234f14b562aebf3e2a7addf374),
whose package metadata identifies version 5.3.0. They are embedded locally and
are not downloaded at runtime. The following common SIL Open Font License 1.1
text applies to both font families.

### Inter

- Distribution: [`@fontsource/inter`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@fontsource/inter)
- Exact Fontsource snapshot: [Inter files at `1fdb61d`](https://github.com/fontsource/font-files/tree/1fdb61d90d0e5a234f14b562aebf3e2a7addf374/fonts/google/inter)
- Upstream source: [rsms/inter](https://github.com/rsms/inter)
- Copyright 2016 The Inter Project Authors (https://github.com/rsms/inter)
- License: SIL Open Font License 1.1.

### Noto Sans
